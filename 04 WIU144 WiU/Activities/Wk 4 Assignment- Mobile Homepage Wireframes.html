My World - Mobile Wireframes

Mobile device usage has increased dramatically in recent years. This increased usage, coupled with an increasingly competitive market has made the purchase of mobile devices more affordable. Today, more people access the web using their mobile device than they do using their home desktop computers. This is largely due to the increase in processing and connectivity available on most modern mobile devices. As a designer, it is your job to focus on these users. Know what their primary needs are, what they want and expect, and how much they are willing to tolerate, in order to create a more effective and enjoyable mobile experience. The information discussed and provided should help get you started. THIS EXERCISE IS DUE AT THE BEGINNING OF LECTURE 8.
